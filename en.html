About BUND24

I am Fang, a self-employed entrepreneur specialising in the exhibition and sale of vases and art objects inspired by Chinese culture. Passionate about Asian art and culture, I carefully select unique pieces in porcelain, ceramics, and jade, combining refinement, tradition, and elegance. Through my work, I aim to share the beauty and history of these exceptional objects, whether they are intended to enrich a collection, enhance an interior, or serve as an elegant gift. Contact me to learn more about my collection or to arrange a visit. I would be delighted to assist you in your search and introduce you to my artistic world.

The products

Blue cloisonné vase: Adorned with plum blossoms, a symbol of elegance and renewal in Chinese art.

Enamelled porcelain vase: Decorated with peonies and a bird, representing prosperity and beauty.

Minimalist black vase: Sleek design with an engraved golden feather, combining modernity and refinement.

Blue and white porcelain vases: Inspired by the Ming dynasty, featuring traditional Chinese landscapes.

Blue and white porcelain vase: Traditional motifs with fish, symbols of prosperity and abundance in China.

Celadon green enamel vase: Decorated with refined floral patterns, inspired by imperial Qing dynasty porcelains.
